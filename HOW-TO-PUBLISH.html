Publish your new Golden Axis website

Before publishing:
1. Replace the PHOTO SLOT boxes with real project photos (save images in this folder and swap the placeholder div for an <img> tag — or send Claude the photos and it will do it).
2. Read every page once — it's your company; the words should sound like you.

Option A — Cloudflare Pages (free, recommended)

  1. Create a free account at pages.cloudflare.com
  2. Choose "Upload assets" (direct upload), drag this entire folder in, and deploy
  3. Add your custom domain goldenaxisconstruction.com under Custom domains and follow the DNS instructions

Option B — Netlify (free, equally good)

  1. Create a free account at netlify.com
  2. Drag this folder onto the dashboard ("Deploy manually")
  3. Site settings → Domain management → add your custom domain

After it's live

  1. Verify the domain at search.google.com/search-console and submit sitemap.xml
  2. Test a page at search.google.com/test/rich-results — business, license and FAQ schema should be detected
  3. Update your Google Business Profile website link, then continue the SEO Action Plan

Domain note

Your domain currently points at the Google AI Studio app. Publishing on Cloudflare/Netlify means updating the domain's DNS to the new host — both platforms show exactly which records to set. Keep the old app until the new site is confirmed live.
