QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a bathroom remodel cost in San Jose?

Quality full remodels typically run $35,000–$90,000+ depending on size, plumbing changes, and finish level. Powder rooms less; primary suites with layout changes more. You get an exact scope and price, not a guess.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most run 4–8 weeks of construction once materials are on hand. We order everything before demo so your home isn't torn up while waiting on a tub.

Will my house be a construction zone?

We contain the work area with dust protection, use floor protection on paths, and clean daily. You'll get a weekly written update, and our crew hours are agreed in advance.

Can you make my shower curbless / add heated floors?

Yes — both are common requests and both depend on structure and slopes, which we confirm during the site visit.
