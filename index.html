ABOUT GOLDEN AXIS

Your trusted partner in custom design & construction

Golden Axis was built on a simple belief — homeowners deserve a contractor they can actually trust. Someone who shows up on time, communicates clearly, and finishes what they start. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we've earned that trust one project at a time.

We're a Bay Area-based team serving San Jose, Oakland, San Francisco, Fremont, Palo Alto, Santa Clara, and Paso Robles. What sets us apart isn't just the quality of our work — it's that we handle design, construction, and material sourcing all under one roof, saving our clients time, money, and stress.

EXECUTION METHOD

Our working process

A streamlined, phase-by-phase approach where design accuracy meets absolute cost transparency.

Pre-Construction & Design

We translate your goals into detailed 3D models and precise layouts before breaking ground — eliminating build-time ambiguity. Deliverable: tailored scope, brief, and permit-ready set.

Cost Control & Sourcing

A fixed-scope proposal with exact inclusions, exclusions, and allowances — plus direct-to-manufacturer sourcing that unlocks luxury savings.

Active Construction & Craft

High-fidelity execution by specialized artisans: daily site logs, photo documentation, and a weekly written update every Friday.

Delivery, Handover & Beyond

Meticulous detailing, white-glove setup, a completed punch list, written warranty, and multi-year care.
