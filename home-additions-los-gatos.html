LOCAL KNOWLEDGE

Building additions in Los Gatos

The Town of Los Gatos reviews additions for neighborhood compatibility, and hillside properties bring their own grading, drainage, and access considerations — we've planned for all of it. Older homes near downtown may need foundation and electrical upgrades to support new square footage; we identify that during design, not during construction. Second-story additions get careful structural review so the result is solid and quiet.

QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What does an addition cost in Los Gatos?

Ground-floor additions typically run $700–$1,100+ per square foot all-in depending on complexity and finishes; second stories more due to structural work. Your fixed-scope proposal breaks it down line by line.

Can we live in the house during construction?

Usually yes for single-room additions — we seal the work zone and coordinate utility switchovers. Larger projects, we'll give you an honest answer during design.

Do additions in Los Gatos need special approval?

Additions require building permits, and some — especially hillside or larger projects — get additional planning review. We manage the full approval path.

Will the addition match the original house?

That's the standard we design to: matched rooflines, siding, windows, and trim profiles so the addition disappears into the architecture.
