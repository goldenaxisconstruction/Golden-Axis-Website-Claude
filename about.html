OUR STORY

Design, construction & sourcing under one roof

With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we've earned our clients' trust one project at a time. Golden Axis Construction is led by founder and CEO Renan Serrano, a California-licensed General Building Contractor (CSLB #1157113) who pairs a solid foundation in construction engineering with a lifelong appreciation for premium architecture and global material sourcing.

What sets us apart isn't just the quality of our work — it's that we handle design, construction, and material sourcing all under one roof, saving our clients time, money, and stress. Every project runs on a written scope, a milestone schedule, signed change orders, and a weekly update you can rely on.

We serve San Jose, Oakland, San Francisco, Fremont, Palo Alto, Santa Clara, Paso Robles, and the greater Bay Area.
